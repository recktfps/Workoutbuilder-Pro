# Privacy Policy for WorkoutBuilder Pro

**Last Updated: January 12, 2026**

## Overview

WorkoutBuilder Pro ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection & Storage

### Local Data Only
All your workout data, programs, exercises, and personal records are stored **locally on your device** using SQLite database. We do NOT:
- Upload your workout data to our servers
- Store your personal information in the cloud
- Share your data with third parties
- Track your workouts remotely

### What Data We Store (Locally)
- Workout history (exercises, sets, reps, weights)
- Custom workout programs
- Exercise library (default + any custom exercises you create)
- Personal records and achievements
- App preferences (weight units, rest timer settings)

### Data You Control
- **Export**: You can export all your data at any time (CSV, PDF)
- **Delete**: Uninstalling the app deletes all local data
- **Privacy**: Your data never leaves your device

## Subscription Management

We use **RevenueCat** to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect:
- Anonymous user ID (generated, not tied to personal info)
- Purchase history and subscription status
- Device type and OS version (for subscription validation)

RevenueCat Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

## Analytics (Optional)

We use **Firebase Analytics** (Google) to understand app usage and improve the experience. This is **anonymous** and includes:
- App opens and session duration
- Feature usage (which screens are most used)
- Crash reports (to fix bugs)

**No personal data, workout data, or identifiable information is sent to analytics.**

You can opt out of analytics in Settings.

Firebase Privacy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

## No Account Required

WorkoutBuilder Pro does NOT require:
- Account creation
- Email address
- Phone number
- Social media login

You can use the app completely anonymously.

## Third-Party Services

### RevenueCat (Subscription Management)
- Purpose: Process in-app purchases and manage subscriptions
- Data: Anonymous user ID, purchase status
- Privacy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

### Firebase Analytics (Optional)
- Purpose: Understand app usage to improve experience
- Data: Anonymous usage statistics
- Privacy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
- Opt-Out: Available in Settings

## Children's Privacy

WorkoutBuilder Pro is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided data, please contact us.

## Data Security

- All data stored locally is encrypted using iOS/Android system encryption
- No data transmitted over the internet (except subscription validation)
- No cloud storage or backups (unless you use device backup)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all your data within the app
- **Export**: Export your data (Settings > Data > Export)
- **Delete**: Uninstall the app to delete all data
- **Opt-Out**: Disable analytics in Settings

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted in the app and on our website. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.

## App Store Privacy Labels

### Data Linked to You

### Data Not Linked to You

- Crash Data (to fix bugs)

**We believe your workout data is YOUR data.**
