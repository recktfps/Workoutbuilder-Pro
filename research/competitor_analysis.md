# Competitor Analysis: WorkoutBuilder Pro

## Overview

The workout tracking app space is competitive but fragmented, with no single dominant player owning more than 15-20% market share. Each major competitor has significant strengths AND exploitable weaknesses. WorkoutBuilder Pro has a clear opportunity to position between their gaps.
